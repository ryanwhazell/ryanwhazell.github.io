Current Session

  1. Watchposts - Guard towers and posts across the roads and entrances to and from Drellin's Ferry.
  2. The Green - A grassy field at the center of Drellin's Ferry commercial square.
  3. Town Speaker's House - Home of Norro Wiston.
  4. The Green Apple - Local tap house. Rooms not available, but cheap food and drink.
  5. Morlin's Smithy - The town smith. Offers some weapons, armor, and other metal goods.
  6. Shrine - Local shrine dedicated to Pelor. Run by Brother Derny. Offers scrolls, potions, and healing.
  7. House of Sertieren the Wise - A noble manor overlooking the river. Home of local wizard, Sertieren.
  8. Old Toll House - Used as dungeon, courtroom, guard barracks, and town hall. (Knowledge History Required for more info)
  9. Armory - Provides armor, weapons, and general supplies for the local milita and guard.
  10. The Old Bridge Inn - Larger and somewhat busier than The Green Apple, and offers high quality rooms.
  11. Jarett's Sundries - General supplies and goods.
  12. Delora's Livery Stable - Offers mounts and gear, and stables for the night.
  13. Iormel's Warehouse - Offers storage, primarily for traveling merchants.
  14. The Ferry - Operated by Dragthar, great-grandnephew of Drellin himself (Knowledge history for more on Drellin). Ferries folk across the river for a modest toll.
  15. The Dwarfbridge - A dilapedated and unusable stone bridge(Knowledge History Check Required)
  16. Gausler's Brewhouse - A brewery run by a half-orc.
  17. Jendar's Warehouse - A competing warehouse, cutting into Iormel's business.
  18. Sterrel's Provisioning - General goods catered to traveling merchants.
  19. The Old Ones - A small clearing nearby in the woods. (Knowledge Local/Nature, Information Gathering check required)
